# <p align="center">UCI HAR Tidy Dataset Codebook</p>
<br>
___
**Subject**
<br>
Number designating the subject performing the experiment. Subject number range from 1 to 30. Subjects 2, 4, 9, 10, 12, 13, 18, 20, and 24 belong to the "test" subset, while the other subjects belong to the "training" subset.
___
**Activity**
<br>
Describes the activity performed by the subject during the experiment. Subject may be "LAYING", "SITTING", "STANDING", "WALKING", "WALKING\_DOWNSTAIRS", or "WALKING\_UPSTAIRS".
___
**tBodyAcc-mean()-X**
<br>
Denotes the mean value estimated from the body acceleration time signals along the x-axis. The given value is the mean of all measurements per activity per subject. The value is in units of radians per second squared and is normalized and bounded within [-1,1].
